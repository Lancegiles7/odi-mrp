Behaviour notes

  • View toggle — "From forecast" shows the raw forecast (including pipefill). "From production plan" shows what you're actually scheduled to make. URL is ?source=production (default) or ?source=forecast.
  • Row click — anywhere on the ingredient row toggles the product-level breakdown open/closed. Each child row shows one product's monthly contribution, with "X g/unit" to remind you of the BOM rate.
  • Shortfall highlighting — a month cell goes red when cumulative demand up to that month exceeds opening stock. Once purchase orders are wired later, their arrivals will be added to the balance.
  • Opening stock — identical pattern to the Production schedule: dashed-border empty state, save-on-blur, leave blank to clear the override.
  • Units — each ingredient shows its own unit (kg, g, each). Mixed-unit roll-ups happen per ingredient, not across them. "Demand 12mo" tile breaks out kg vs sachets.

Implementation (for reference)

  • New migration: add opening_stock_override to ingredients (nullable numeric).
  • New lib/ingredient-demand.ts: given (products, BOMs, demand|production), aggregate per-ingredient per-month totals and per-product contributions. Handles g ↔ kg conversion.
  • New page app/(dashboard)/ingredients/demand/page.tsx, new row component components/ingredients/ingredient-demand-row.tsx.
  • Server action updateIngredientOpeningStock(ingredientId, value) — mirrors the product override action.
  • Sidebar link: "Ingredient demand" under the Planning section, next to "Demand" and "Production".
